// the mission

Built for the
left behind.

A dump truck dispatcher. A phone. An AI. A Raspberry Pi in a closet. This is what $0 cloud costs looks like.

800 million Windows users are about to lose security updates. They can't afford enterprise software. They don't have IT departments. They have families, jobs, bills — and they deserve the same AI infrastructure that corporations pay millions for.

Angel Cloud is the proof of concept. One Pi 5. One dispatcher in Alabama. 17 bots running 24/7. RAG search, vector storage, local LLMs, Discord bots, financial tools, medical billing AI, creative writing pipelines — all of it on $80 of hardware, zero monthly cloud fees, fully private.

If it runs here, it runs everywhere. This is the architecture for the next billion people who need AI that respects their data, their budget, and their privacy.
